Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 Hour
  • Serves: 18
Ingredients Quantity
Phyllo Dough 12 Sheets
Chopped nuts ¾ cup
Chopped Pistachio ¼ cup
Butter 3 tbsp
Sugar Syrup ½ cup

Preparation:

  1. Unwrap the phyllo dough carefully and separate them as three layers come together.
  2. Brush melted butter on top.
  3. Now add a table spoon of crushed nuts mix on one side of the phyllo on length wise.
  4. Fold it three times and add another tablespoon of nuts at the end of folding as shown in the picture.
  5. Fold it again and add another teaspoon of crushed nuts; fold it completely.
  6. Place it in to butter greased baking tray as the folding end touches the bottom.
  7. In such a way, make the remaining three rolls and arrange it on the baking tray.
  8. Brush butter on top of it evenly.
  9. Run a knife through the baking tray and cut the rolls in to small roll sized pieces.
  10. Add some crushed pistachio on top of each roll.
  11. Bake it in a preheated oven at 180 degree for 20-25 minutes until the top gets a nice golden in color.
  12. Mean while make the sugar syrup.
  13. Boil all the ingredients for few minutes until one string consistency is reached.
  14. Allow it to cool a bit (keep it hot but not in the boiling temperature)
  15. Pour the hot syrup over the baked baklava.
  16. Allow it to cools down completely.
  17. Serve hot and enjoy the melting crispiness !!!
